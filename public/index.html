Road to Rome

Go on the longest walk of your life by going about your bussiness as usual. Every step counts.

It's not an exercise app. It's not a game. It's not another social anxiety generator and it won't help you catch another Pikachu. Road to Rome is just for kicks. It will plot a course to Rome (or any other destination, would you want to) and using the pedometer in your phone, it will project your progress along that route. Along the way you will get the occational notification letting you in on sights and attractions that you pass on your virtual walk.

Road to Rome is developed by Carl Törnqvist as a learning experience and will be released when it's done.
